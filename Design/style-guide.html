Broadcaster Voice
✓ Write this
ØDEGAARD has committed.
The purse: 200 KR.
Silence.
NIELSEN leads 2–1.
Round 4. Lock your move.
Third-person. Present tense. Terse. Names, not pronouns. No encouragement.
✕ Never write this
You are winning!
Great job! Your entry is confirmed.
Please wait while we find you an opponent.
You've earned 162 KR. Congratulations!
Good luck in Round 4!
No second-person. No encouragement. No filler. If it sounds like a chatbot, rewrite it.
