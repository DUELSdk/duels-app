## Features

### Opponent Recent History
Pre-game or in-lobby: optional view of what your opponent played in their last 5–10 games.

- Shows game type, win/loss, and outcome (not full sequence — just result)
- Opt-in display — player can choose to look or not
- Adds scouting layer: is opponent on a streak? Tilting? Playing safe?
- Relevant for all games but most impactful in Card Duel (pattern reading)
- Does not show card sequences — only results. Full sequences would break sealed format integrity.
- Toggle: show/hide. Some players won't want to be scouted.
