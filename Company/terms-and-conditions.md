## 4. Entry Fees and Prize Purse

### How it works

To enter a match, both players pay an entry fee. The two entry fees combine to form the prize purse. The winner receives the purse minus the platform fee. In the event of a tie, the purse is split equally between both players after the platform fee is deducted.

### Platform fee

DUELS charges a platform fee on each match:
- 1v1 matches: 10% of the prize purse
- Tournament matches: 15% of the prize purse

### Entry fee tiers

Entry fee tiers are displayed in the platform at time of match entry. Minimum entry fee: 10 DKK. Maximum entry fee: 500 DKK per match (higher tiers available in Elite Rooms at 1,000 DKK+, subject to eligibility).

### Sweetened challenges

A player may issue a sweetened challenge by adding to the prize purse beyond their symmetric entry fee. The platform fee is calculated on the symmetric portion only. The sweetening player's additional contribution is a voluntary structural cost — no platform fee applies to it.

### Result screen

After each match, the result screen displays: prize purse, entry fee paid, and net gain or loss. This is the authoritative record of the financial outcome.

## 6. Fair Play and Anti-Cheat

### Prohibited conduct

The following are strictly prohibited:

- Use of automated tools, bots, or scripts during matches
- Collusion with an opponent to manipulate match outcomes
- Account sharing or playing on behalf of another person
- Any form of match-fixing or deliberate underperformance for financial gain
- Exploiting platform bugs or vulnerabilities for advantage

### Detection

DUELS uses server-side game state rendering, input fingerprinting, timing analysis, statistical performance monitoring, and stake pattern analysis to detect prohibited conduct. MitID authentication prevents anonymous accounts.

### Enforcement tiers

| Tier | Trigger | Consequence |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Warning | First detected anomaly | Formal warning logged to account |
| Restriction | Repeated anomalies | Low-stake lockout + tournament ban |
| Ban | Confirmed violation | Account terminated, funds forfeited, winnings clawed back |
| Referral | Financial fraud confirmed | Referred to Politiet under §279 straffeloven (bedrageri) |

### Clawback

Winnings obtained through prohibited conduct are subject to clawback regardless of withdrawal status. DUELS reserves the right to reverse transactions and recover funds. This right survives account termination.

### Legal basis

Obtaining financial gain through deception on DUELS constitutes bedrageri under §279 of the Danish Criminal Code (straffeloven). DUELS will pursue criminal referral in confirmed cases.

## 7. Forfeit Rules

A player may forfeit a match at any time. Forfeiting awards the prize purse to the opponent (minus platform fee). Forfeiting does not entitle the forfeiting player to a refund of their entry fee.

**Forfeit cooldown:** 3 forfeits within any 24-hour period triggers a 30-minute cooldown on rooms with an entry fee of 100 DKK or more.
