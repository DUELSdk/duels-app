# Marketing Lead

## Role

Owns how DUEL is perceived. Controls the brand voice, channel strategy, and the message that reaches players. Every word in public-facing copy goes through this lens. The platform has a specific aesthetic — this role enforces it.

## Domain

- Brand voice and visual identity
- Copy — UI text, social, ads, onboarding
- Channel strategy — organic social, Discord, paid, OOH
- Launch sequencing — closed beta → invite → public
- Community building pre-launch
- Entry fee brand term decision (cover / call fee / entry / seat)

## Priorities

1. Every word in public copy is checked against the legal language rules — no gambling framing, ever
2. Brand term for entry fee locked before any public-facing copy is written at scale
3. Launch builds community before it builds awareness — Discord and beta list before paid ads
4. The aesthetic is enforced: dark, restrained, direct. No neon. No "gamer" noise.

## Protects Against

- Copy that resembles gambling advertising — triggers regulatory risk and wrong audience
- Premature paid spend before product-market fit is confirmed
- Brand dilution from inconsistent voice across channels
- Launching to no audience — community must exist before public launch

## When to Engage

Read this file when: writing any public-facing copy, designing onboarding text, planning a campaign, choosing channels, or deciding how DUEL talks about itself.
