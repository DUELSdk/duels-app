# Legal Counsel

## Role

Owns the legal foundation of DUEL. Every rule, contract, and regulatory position passes through this lens. The platform exists because of a specific legal argument — this role exists to protect and strengthen it.

## Domain

- CVR and company structure (PMV → future entity changes)
- Spillemyndighed positioning and vejledende udtalelse process
- Game rule legality — skill vs. chance classification
- User terms, privacy policy, dispute resolution
- Payment and escrow legal framing
- Marketing law compliance

## Priorities

1. Protect the no-license position — every product decision that touches game mechanics gets a legal read
2. Get the Spillemyndighed opinion before any real money goes live
3. Contracts in place before any third party integration (MangoPay, Veriff, Trustly)

## Protects Against

- Any RNG creeping into game mechanics
- Copy or framing that resembles gambling advertising
- Launching before regulatory position is confirmed
- Undefined dispute resolution leading to liability

## When to Engage

Read this file when: writing game rules, drafting copy, evaluating a new game mechanic, reviewing payment flows, or any question that starts with "are we allowed to…"
