# Team Event Format

Auto-assigned teams (Blue vs Red), 50/50 split for equal odds.

Two formats in consideration — see legal risk notes at bottom.

---

## Format A — Pure Team Tournament (Higher Legal Risk)

One fee. Entire purse team-dependent.

---

## Structure

- One entry fee
- Play X matches vs opposing team only (Blue vs Red matchmaking)
- Max downside always = entry fee, regardless of performance

---

## Payouts

- Winning team takes 95% of full pot, performance-weighted
- Your share = your wins / total winning team wins
- Calculated dynamically at end of event
- Losing team gets nothing from pot
- Exceptional losing team performers (e.g. 5-0) get platform credits — not from pot
- Platform takes 5% as entry fee on top (e.g. 105kr entry, 100kr to pot)

---

## Live Ticker

During event, players see:
- Current projected payout (updates after every match)
- Live team score — Blue 847 vs Red 791
- Live pot size

## Format B — Dual-Fee (Lower Legal Risk)

Two separate fees per event:

| Fee | Amount (example) | What it buys |
|-----|-----------------|--------------|
| Match fee | 100kr | Your individual 1v1 matches |
| Team bonus fee | 20kr | Entry into team pot |

**Match fee payouts:**
- Win: ~1.8x match fee per win (e.g. 180kr)
- Loss: lose match fee
- Resolved individually — team outcome irrelevant
- Max downside = match fee losses + team bonus fee

**Team bonus pot:**
- Winning team takes 95%, performance-weighted (your wins / total winning team wins)
- Losing team gets nothing from pot
- Exceptional losing performers get platform credits instead

**Why legally cleaner:**
Core earnings (match fee layer) are 100% individual skill — team outcome only controls the small bonus pot. Legal exposure isolated to 20kr layer, not full purse.

---

## Legal Risk Comparison

| | Format A | Format B |
|--|----------|----------|
| Core payout depends on teammates | Yes | No |
| Collective outcome dependency | Full purse | Bonus only |
| Spillemyndigheden risk | Higher | Lower |
| Player experience simplicity | Simpler | Slightly complex |
